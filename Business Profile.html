About

Acme Robotics designs and commissions robotic palletizing, depalletizing, and end-of-line integration systems for food & beverage, automotive, and consumer goods plants across the Midwest. We are a Fanuc Platinum Partner and a Rockwell Recognized System Integrator. Our 86-person engineering team handles concept-through-commissioning under one roof, and we contract additional controls engineers through Automate America for peak load.
