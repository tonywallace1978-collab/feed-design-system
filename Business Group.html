About this group

A peer network of industrial automation engineers, integrators, and plant managers working across the five-state Great Lakes region. We share contracts, swap site tips, run quarterly meetups in rotating cities, and keep a referrals list for short-notice work. Membership is reviewed quarterly. We do not allow recruiters or sales pitches in the feed.
