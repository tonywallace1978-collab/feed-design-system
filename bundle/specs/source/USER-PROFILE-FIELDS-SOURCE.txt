Flow
Creating a Profile:
1. The user fills out a registration form with their email, password, first name, last name, and phone number for two-factor authentication.
2. The user receives a verification code via SMS to complete the two-factor authentication process.
3. Upon verification, the user logs in and is prompted to complete their profile by filling in personal, professional, and financial information.
4. The user can upload secure personal files (ID Proof, Background Check, Drug Test, etc.) for verification.
Editing a Profile:
1. The user navigates to their profile settings.
2. The user updates the necessary fields and saves changes.
3. For sensitive information, like phone number or secure personal files, the user must verify changes through two-factor authentication.
Profile Completion:
1. Users are prompted to complete additional fields when they attempt to post jobs or apply for contracts.
2. Incomplete profiles are flagged, and users receive notifications to complete their profiles.
Availability Management:
1. Users mark themselves as available or unavailable.
2. If marked available, the status remains active for one week.
3. Users receive notifications to renew their availability status after one week.
Deleting a Profile:
1. Users have the option to delete their profile permanently.
2. Users must confirm the deletion multiple times and are warned that they will not be able to recover any profile data once deleted.
3. A final confirmation step includes a message stating: "Are you sure you want to permanently delete your profile? This action cannot be undone."

Turning Products/Services into Paid Ads:
1. Users can select any product or service they have listed.
2. With the push of a button, the product or service can be turned into a paid advertisement.
3. Users are prompted to set the budget and duration for the ad.
* Tooltip: "Promote your products or services by turning them into paid advertisements with just a click."
Boosting Profiles:
1. Users can boost their profiles for $50 per week.
2. Boosted profiles receive increased exposure by appearing once every time any person scrolls through the complete feed.
* Tooltip: "Boost your profile for increased visibility and exposure."
Use Case
Scenario: John, a skilled contractor, wants to find new job opportunities on AutomateAmerica.com. He creates a detailed profile, including his skills, work experience, and certifications. This helps potential clients understand his qualifications and consider him for their projects. Additionally, John lists his specialized consulting service and converts it into a paid ad to increase visibility. He also boosts his profile for a week to ensure it gets maximum exposure. Benefit: Having a comprehensive profile increases John’s visibility and attractiveness to potential clients, leading to more job offers and opportunities. The ability to turn services into paid ads and boost his profile helps John market his consulting services effectively and ensures his profile stands out to potential clients.
