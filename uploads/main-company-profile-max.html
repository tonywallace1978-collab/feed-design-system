About

Dominion Manufacturing Group is a full-service systems integrator headquartered in Sterling Heights, Michigan. Founded in 2011, we specialize in robotic welding, PLC controls, vision-guided assembly, and turnkey automation solutions for automotive OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers. Our 47 engineers and technicians operate across 5 business groups spanning Michigan, Ohio, and Kentucky. We have successfully delivered over 200 automation projects for clients including General Motors, Ford, Stellantis, Toyota, BMW, and Honda, with a 97% on-time delivery rate and a 4.8-star customer rating.
