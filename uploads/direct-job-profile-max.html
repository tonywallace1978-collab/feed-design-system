Job Description
Axiom Controls Inc. is seeking a Senior PLC Programmer to join our expanding automation team in Grand Rapids, Michigan. In this role, you will design, develop, and commission PLC-based control systems for industrial automation projects across automotive, food processing, and pharmaceutical manufacturing sectors. You will lead the programming effort on multi-vendor platforms including Allen-Bradley ControlLogix, Siemens S7-1500/TIA Portal, and Rockwell Automation safety systems. Responsibilities include developing structured text and ladder logic programs from P&ID and electrical schematics, integrating HMI/SCADA systems (FactoryTalk, WinCC), configuring industrial networks (EtherNet/IP, PROFINET, Modbus TCP), performing FAT/SAT testing, and providing on-site commissioning support. This is a permanent W-2 position with full benefits, reporting to the Director of Engineering.
