About This Group

Sure Controls Division is the PLC and robot programming arm of Dominion Manufacturing Group, headquartered in Sterling Heights, Michigan. We specialize in Allen-Bradley ControlLogix, FANUC and ABB robotic systems, Cognex vision integration, and Siemens TIA Portal development for automotive body shop, paint, and assembly applications. Our team of 12 has delivered 38 contracts across General Motors, Ford, Stellantis, Toyota, and BMW facilities in Michigan, Ohio, Indiana, and Kentucky. We maintain a 98% on-time delivery rate and a 4.7-star average rating from customers.
